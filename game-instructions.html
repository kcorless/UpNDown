How to Play "The Game"

Quick Start

  1. Create an account or sign in
  2. Click "New Game" to start a game (you'll get a 4-character game ID)
  3. Share the game ID with friends who want to join
  4. Other players can join using the "Join Game" option
  5. The game begins when all players have joined

Game Rules

Goal

Work together to play all cards from your hands onto the four piles. You win when all cards are played!

Setup

  • Two ascending piles (starting at 1)
  • Two descending piles (starting at 100)
  • Each player gets 6-8 cards (depends on player count)

On Your Turn

  • Must play at least 2 cards
  • Can play on any pile in the correct direction
  • Ascending piles: Must play higher numbers
  • Descending piles: Must play lower numbers
  • Special Rule: Can play exactly 10 less on ascending or 10 more on descending

Communication Features

  • Use the chat to coordinate with other players
  • When it's not your turn, you can mark piles with "Like" or "Love" to suggest moves
  • Watch for other players' pile preferences to plan your strategy

Tips for Success

  • Communicate with your team - use the chat and pile preferences
  • Plan ahead - watch what cards other players might have
  • Use the "10 rule" strategically to keep piles accessible
  • Don't rush - coordinate with your team before making moves
